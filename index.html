🌿 ABOUT 🌿

As a teen, I had a strong interest towards design and programming. I started my college career studying both Graphic Design and Computer Science. But as time went on, I started to focus more on Computer Science. Despite this, my passion for the arts remained and I continued to do art on the side while studying CS.

My interest in art and design has drawn me towards web developement and UI/UX Design. I taught myself how to work with HTML, CSS, React and JavaScript. A fun learning experience which allowed me to code this very website from scratch.

I hope to become a full-stack developer with a focus on UI/UX design!
