🎃 2020 Halloween GameJam 🎃

The Pumpkin Princess

Pumpkin Princess: Curse of the Pumpkin Patch is a project that I hold near and dear to my heart. On October 2020, HackUTD annouced their first ever GameJam. Which, in the spirit of October, was themed appropriately after Halloween.

Coincidentally, that semester also happened to be the my first semester at UTD. After having heard all sorts of stories from my community college professors about Hackathons, I was more than excited to participate in my very first one ✨


Character sheet and concept art

One of the best parts of the project was the fact that my teammates gave a lot of creative freedom 🤩

As the person in charge of the art direction of the game, I got to be as creative as possible when it came to the designs of our main character (working name: PP) and all the monsters within the game.
